# Cybersecurity Audit Practice Portfolio

A collection of fictional security audit scenarios designed to practice risk assessment, controls gap analysis, and compliance mapping across multiple frameworks and industries.

Each scenario consists of two documents — a **Risk Assessment Report** and a **Controls & Compliance Checklist** — mirroring the structure of a real-world security audit engagement.

## Skills Demonstrated

- Asset inventory and scoping
- Risk scoring and justification
- Controls gap analysis (technical, administrative, physical)
- Multi-framework compliance mapping (HIPAA, PCI DSS, GLBA, NIST CSF, NIST SP 800-171, CMMC)
- Threat modeling and APT threat intelligence contextualization (MITRE ATT&CK)
- POA&M development and SPRS scoring (CMMC / DoD environment)
- Prioritized remediation planning

---

## How to Use These

Each scenario is a standalone practice exercise. The recommended workflow:

1. Read the **Risk Assessment Report** to understand the organization, assets, and identified risks
2. Complete the **Controls & Compliance Checklist** — mark each control Yes or No based on the report
3. Write your **Recommendations** section at the bottom of the checklist
4. Compare your answers and reasoning against the scenario's findings

> These are fictional scenarios created for educational and portfolio purposes only.
